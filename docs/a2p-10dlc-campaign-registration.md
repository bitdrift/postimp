## Campaign Description

This campaign powers an SMS-based social media management service. Users text photos and descriptions to create Instagram posts. The service replies with AI-generated captions for review. Users can approve, request revisions, or cancel posts via SMS. Messages also include signup links, post preview URLs, publishing confirmations, and help information.

## End-User Consent

End users opt in by first texting the Post Imp phone number, which sends them a signup link. Opt-in occurs on the signup page at https://postimp.com/signup, where users must check a consent checkbox before creating their account. The checkbox states: "By signing up, you consent to receive SMS messages from Post Imp (e.g. draft captions, post confirmations, account notifications). Consent is not a condition of purchase. Msg & data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for assistance." The signup button is disabled until the checkbox is checked. Privacy policy is available at https://postimp.com/privacy and terms of service at https://postimp.com/terms.
